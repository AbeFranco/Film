The Story

He sings. He waves. He makes mornings better.

Meet Mr. Curtis—Midland’s singing crossing guard. Rain or shine, his smile shows up before the sun does.

This short video showcases the spirit of a man who’s not just keeping kids safe, but reminding us all how to show up for each other—with joy.

Director's Note

Joy is a Strategy.

In a world obsessed with "content," I look for Source. Mr. Curtis isn't just waving traffic; he is operating from a deep well of purpose.

As a filmmaker, I don't manufacture stories. I find the people who are already living them. This film is an examination of what happens when you decide to dance even when the world expects you to just stand still.
