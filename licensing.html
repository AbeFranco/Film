Licensing

What Licensing Means

Licensing grants permission to use the film internally as a recurring resource.

  • Showing it to multiple groups
  • Returning to it over time
  • Building facilitated experiences around it

There are no subscriptions.
No portals.
No usage tracking systems.

This mirrors how schools and organizations already operate.

How Licensing Is Enforced

  • Clear license language
  • Clear invoice descriptions
  • Institutional good faith

No DRM. No policing.

Pricing

Entry License — $495–$750

  • One group or cohort
  • Low friction
  • All materials included

Best for one-time programs or pilots.

Standard License — $1,500

  • Up to three cohorts
  • Two-year term
  • Repeat internal use

This is where most organizations land.

Signature Film License — $3,000–$5,000

  • Organization-wide internal use
  • Unlimited screenings
  • Perpetual license
  • Curriculum updates included
  • Optional light customization

This tier reflects freedom, reuse, and long-term permission — not film length.

Payment Flow

  • Under $1,500 → secure checkout link
  • $1,500+ → Stripe payment link or invoice
  • $5,000 → invoice (card, ACH, or check)

Links are often sent during the call to preserve momentum.
